# Google Play Console Registration Guide

To publish your app "Afro Arab" on the Play Store, you need to create a Developer Account. Follow these steps:

## 1. Prerequisites
- A Google Account (Gmail).
- A valid credit or debit card (Visa, Mastercard, Amex, Discover).
- **$25 USD** one-time registration fee.
- A valid government-issued ID (Passport, Driving License) for identification.

## 2. Step-by-Step Registration
1.  **Go to the Console**: Open [Play Console](https://play.google.com/console/signup).
2.  **Select Account Type**: Most individuals choose "Personal". For a registered business, choose "Organization".
3.  **Enter Developer Info**: Provide your developer name (this will be visible on the Play Store), contact name, and physical address.
4.  **Verify Email & Phone**: Google will send verification codes to your email and phone number.
5.  **Review Agreements**: Read and accept the Developer Distribution Agreement.
6.  **Pay the Fee**: Enter your card details and pay the **$25** one-time fee.
7.  **Identify Verification**: Google will ask you to upload a photo of your ID. This can take 24-48 hours to be approved.

## 3. Important Details for Later
While your account is being verified, we will continue preparing the app:
- **Package Name**: Currently set to `com.afroarab.app`. Let me know if you want to change this (e.g., `com.medexcellence.afroarab`).
- **Icons**: I will need to generate high-resolution icons (512x512) and feature graphics (1024x500).
- **Testing**: Google now requires 20 testers for 14 days for new personal accounts before you can publish to the public.
