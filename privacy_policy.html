Privacy Policy for Afro Arab

Effective Date: March 6, 2026

At Afro Arab, accessible via our mobile application, one of our main priorities is the privacy of our visitors. This Privacy Policy document contains types of information that is collected and recorded by Afro Arab and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

When you register for an account or use our services, we may collect the following information:

  • Personal Details: Name, mobile number, and business type.
  • Documents and Media: Images, screenshots, and PDF files (e.g., business registrations, payment proofs, and official quotes) that you voluntarily upload.
  • Device Information: Basic device details for push notifications and technical troubleshooting.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect in various ways, including to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our platform.
  • Improve, personalize, and expand our services.
  • Understand and analyze how you use our platform.
  • Communicate with you, either directly or through one of our partners, including for customer service and notification of order updates.
  • Send you push notifications and in-app alerts.
  • Protect the security of our users and prevent fraudulent registrations.

3. Data Security & Storage

We value your trust in providing us your Personal Information, thus we are striving to use commercially acceptable means of protecting it. Your data is stored on secure servers (Hostinger VPS) and transmitted via encrypted HTTPS protocols. However, remember that no method of transmission over the internet, or method of electronic storage is 100% secure and reliable, and we cannot guarantee its absolute security.

4. Media and File Access

The app requires permission to access your device's storage and camera to allow the uploading of business documents and screenshots. These files are used solely for verification and business transactions within the Afro Arab ecosystem.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

You have the right to request access to the data we hold about you, or to request that we correct or delete your personal information. If you wish to exercise these rights, please contact the Afro Arab administration.

6. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. Thus, we advise you to review this page periodically for any changes. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
